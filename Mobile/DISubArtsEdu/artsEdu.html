NIKERI Institute

The National Indigenous Knowledge Education Research Innovation (NIKERI) Institute enables Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Australians to access higher education while still being able to maintain family and community obligations.

T he NIKERI Institute offers an extensive range of faculty degrees in a community-based mode of delivery that are relevant, engaging and inclusive for Indigenous students.
