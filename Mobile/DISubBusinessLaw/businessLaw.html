Why choose Deakin?

The Faculty of Business and Law is committed to offering students a rewarding business or legal education, and offering access to industry, professional networks and award-winning courses.

With teaching across three campuses, we prepare our graduates for the careers of the future. Our two schools (Deakin Business School and Deakin Law School) offer courses relevant to contemporary national and global issues.

Innovative

Our innovative teaching methods, state-of-the-art technology and flexible learning strategies give students a truly unique experience.

Students

Our students are taught by renowned academic staff who are engaged in research that informs real-world issues within government, business and community organisations.

Deakin Business School (DBS)

Deakin Business School (DBS) is accredited by AACSB International. This accreditation is awarded to business schools that meet strict standards of quality, academic and professional excellence. Only five per cent of business programs worldwide are AACSB accredited and DBS is one of only three in Victoria. DBS has courses backed by high-quality contemporary research and developed in consultation with industry, meaning students graduate ready for their chosen career.

Schools

Deakin Business School

Through offering internationally renowned courses – including the Bachelor of Commerce and the Master of Business Administration (MBA) – Deakin Business School runs courses that helps students find a place in their chosen fields.

Our work-integrated learning initiatives and overseas opportunities give you the firsthand experience you need to understand business, from within Australia and internationally.

Deakin Law School

Interested in commercial law? With us, you’ll learn everything you’ll need to have a successful career in renowned law firms, whether here in Australia or further afield.

Get real-world legal experience through our legal internship program, and sign up to one of our many international study programs or workplace and community placements. We’ll get your career off to a great start.

Business and Law Development Network (BUiLD)

Deakin's Business and Law Schools are supported by BUiLD, which encourages the schools' research capacity and capability development. BUiLD also promotes research networks within Deakin and externally, and supports student researchers through a range of events and resources.
